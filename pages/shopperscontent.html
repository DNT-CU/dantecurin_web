2022 - Cornershop by Uber

This is a sample of designs and adaptations created for Cornershop by Uber. These products were used to apply the brand's visual identity to their partners or Shoppers, who deliver groceries, supermarket items, and other goods directly to customers' homes.

As Cornershop by Uber sends newsletters with useful content to their Shoppers, there is a need for simplyfing communications for important topics such as tips for picking products or explaining payment rates. So the badges aim to complement the given information. These assets were made using the resources of the design team and adapting them to their brand identity.

Also, we designed on site signage for shoppers and working staff. The topics of these designes ranged from useful tips for shoppers, up to pointing points of distributions and hand-off zones.
